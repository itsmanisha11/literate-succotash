Admission Process

Steps for Applying

01

Registration

Students register for a free counselling session based on their ranking and eligibility criteria.

02

Evaluation

Our team connects with the students to evaluate their profile and preferences. we collaborate to shortlist suitable programs and universities.

03

Offer Letter

Once the profile is complete, student receives the offer letters from the selected universities.

04

Assistance

We assist the student in preparing scholarship applications or obtaining financial assistance to support their education.

05

Visa Preparation

Our team guides the student through the visa application process, ensuring all necessary steps are followed.

06

Visa Approval

It confirms that the students application has been reviewed and accepted, allowing you to enter the destination country.

07

Departure

Providing support by offering guidance on travel arrangements, accommodation, cultural adaptation, and essential documents.

08

Arrival

After arriving in the chosen country, the student completes university registration and accommodation, ready to begin their academic journey.
